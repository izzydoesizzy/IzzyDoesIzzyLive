About

Let me introduce myself.

First off, let's drop the formalities. It's just "Izzy" from now on. I'm a teacher, mentor, and adventurer at heart. I'm possibly the most postitive person on Earth and love inspiring and mentoring others to achieve their personal goals.

In 2014 I leapt out of my comfort zone and left a stable, well-paying career in pursuit of more adventure and meaning in my life.

I do anything I can to learn from others, achieve personal growth, and give of my time as much time as I can to help others through the personal and professional struggles and roadblocks that have slowed me down in the past.

Today, I organize trips around the world for a travelling community of designers, developers, and other creative types called Hacker Paradise.
